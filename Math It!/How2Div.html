How to Divide

Division is the most annoying bit, but one of the more useful ones when you don't have a calculator and need to split a tab at a restaurant! It's a bigger number being split evenly. So if me and my friends went out to eat and the bill was £40, and there were 4 of us, we would divide the 40 by 4, which would give us £10 we would need to pay each to cover the tab
