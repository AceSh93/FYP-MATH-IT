How to Mulpiply

Multiplying is a bit similar to adding, in the sense that the result number is increased by another number. For example, if I had £5 a week of pocket money for 6 weeks, instead of adding 5 on top of 5 six times, i would just times 5 by 6 (5x6=30)

The basic idea of multiplication is repeated addition. For example: 5 × 6 = 5 + 5 + 5 + 5 + 5 + 5= 30
